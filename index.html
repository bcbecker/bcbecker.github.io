Who am I?

As the big letters say, my name is Bryce, and I'm a backend developer. Coding has been a part of my life since I was 18, tinkering with Arduino microcontrollers in my engineering classes. This was followed by using Python for data manipulation as a research student, and now, I've gone full-tilt into developing backend systems in any language necessary. I love learning new techniques and best practices wherever I can, so please don't hesitate to provide feedback. Shoot me an email, check out my Github, or connect with me on LinkedIn!
